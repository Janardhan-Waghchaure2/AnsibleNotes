1. Ansible is an automation tool.

2. Ansible does not require any additional software (agents) to be installed on the managed hosts. 
Instead, Ansible uses standard communication protocols like SSH for Unix-like systems and WinRM for 
Windows systems to connect and execute tasks remotely. Thats why ANSIBLE IS AGENTLESS.

--------
Advantages.

1. Simple and easy to use: due to yaml 
2. Agentless architecture: no agents needs to be installed on remote systems
3. Config management : easy to do tasks like provisiining ,app deployment , infra
   management
4. scalability : easily scales for more. no of servers
